Section 1

Codingal

Codingal is a global online platform offering live, 1:1 after-school coding classes for children and teens aged 6-17, founded by Google and Amazon alumni. It provides a project-based curriculum where students learn to build games, apps, and websites under the guidance of qualified instructors with computer science backgrounds. The platform aims to foster creativity and problem-solving skills through engaging, personalized lessons and is backed by Y Combinator.
